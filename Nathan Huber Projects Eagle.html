Eagle Scout

In the Spring of 2013 I earned the rank of Eagle in the Boy Scouts of America. These are some of the pictures for the project. I renovated a historical cemetery in my home town. The cemetery was in desperate need of repair and was over 200 years old. Many of the headstones have been piled beside the crucifix in the back of the site. Weeds that were taller than me had grown around and on top of many of them. There was over several hundred hours of planning and excuting this project. It could not have been done without the help of my family, friends, and the community.
